What's Tennis Mennis?!

Tennis Mennis is a game of fast-paced tennis action, focusing on unique characters and innovative gameplay mechanics. Choose your favorite character and leave your friends to wallow in defeat!

So, like, who cares?

You do! Tennis Mennis is for anyone who is a cool person and likes quick, competitive action. The game is designed for anyone to be able to jump in and play at any time, so what are you waiting for?

Never Before Seen Tennis Madness

Tight Controls

Tennis Mennis prides itself on smooth-as-warm-butter controls that make for non-stop action and excitement.

Racket Tossing

Characters have the ability to throw and retrieve their rackets during the match, making almost any shot returnable, if you have the finesse for it
